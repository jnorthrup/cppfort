# Product Guidelines

## Communication Tone and Voice

The project's external communication, including documentation, public announcements, and user interaction, will maintain a **Technical and Precise** tone. Emphasis will be placed on accuracy, detail, and technical correctness to ensure clarity and build trust with a highly technical audience.

## Brand Messaging and Visual Identity

Key elements to emphasize in the project's brand messaging and visual identity will be **Innovation and Modernity**. The project aims to showcase its advanced features, forward-thinking design, and strong alignment with modern C++ paradigms.

## Community Contributions and Feedback

Community contributions and feedback will be handled with a philosophy best described as "as if a community of one." This implies a highly focused and streamlined approach, where contributions are integrated with extreme care and precision, prioritizing direct control and specific vision, akin to a sole developer's oversight.

## Documentation Strategy

The primary focus for documentation will be on **Autogeneration**. This approach emphasizes generating comprehensive, up-to-date, and accurate documentation directly from the codebase or formal specifications. This strategy aligns with the technical and precise communication tone and the focus on innovation, ensuring that documentation always reflects the current state of the project with minimal manual overhead.
