GAP 1: Dead Branch Elimination in FIR Dialect
==============================================================================
Status: VERIFIED - not applicable by design
Commit: 2b1021d

Finding:
FIR dialect does not define IfOp or control flow operations. This is correct
by architectural design:
- FIR (Front-IR) represents straight-line expression evaluation
- FIR is "sea of nodes" representation before control flow introduced
- Control flow handled in higher-level dialects (Cpp2, SON)
- Dead branch elimination correctly implemented in SCCPPass.cpp for SON dialect

Investigation confirmed:
- Cpp2 dialect: has Cpp2_IfOp (Cpp2Dialect.td:60)
- SON dialect: has Cpp2SON_IfOp (Cpp2SONDialect.td:422)
- FIR dialect: NO IfOp - only expression ops (Add, Sub, Mul, Div, Cmp, Phi)

Resolution: No implementation required - gap is architectural correctness.
